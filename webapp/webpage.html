SEQUENCE OPTIONS: Select
Add a new sequence; clear the current sequence; pick a different sequence to work on; or delete the current sequence.

Click on the grid to assign notes.
The grid shows a 4 measure sequence.
Your song is made up of these sequences.
You can change the order of sequences by clicking the squares at the bottom of the screen.
You can increase the length of the song (# of sequences) by clicking "longer" and "shorter".
You can change the BPM of the song while its playing.
You can change the scale displayed on the grid to make it easier to play in a scale.
You can select from a set of synth sounds.
